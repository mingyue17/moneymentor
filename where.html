Where to invest

Places young Singaporeans actually start.

These are real, MAS-regulated ways to invest here — from the stock exchange itself to hands-off apps. Pick the lane that fits how much you want to do yourself.

🏛️

The stock market (SGX)

Singapore's stock exchange, where shares, ETFs and REITs are bought and sold. You reach it through a brokerage account (and usually a CDP account).

SGX moomoo Tiger Brokers Interactive Brokers
🤖

Robo-advisors (hands-off)

Apps that build and manage a diversified portfolio for you based on your goals and risk comfort. Low minimums, auto-rebalanced — the easiest starting point.

StashAway Endowus Syfe
🔁

Monthly investing plans

Regular Shares Savings (RSS) plans invest a fixed sum each month into an ETF like the STI ETF — automatic dollar-cost averaging from about S$100/month.

POSB Invest-Saver OCBC BCIP FSMOne
🛡️

Government-backed (safest)

Singapore Savings Bonds and Treasury bills are issued by the Government — your principal is protected. Bought through your bank's app or ATM.

Singapore Savings Bonds SGS & T-bills

Platforms are shown as examples of what's available, not recommendations. Always check a firm on the MAS Investor Alert List before handing over money.
