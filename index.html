The problem

Most young Singaporeans want to invest — but don't know where to start.

Between CPF, SRS, ETFs and a flood of finfluencer takes, getting started feels overwhelming and easy to get wrong. MoneyMentor closes that gap.

🧩

Jargon is a wall

ETF, RSS, dollar-cost averaging — the words alone put beginners off before they even begin.

⚠️

Bad advice is everywhere

Social media is full of hype and scams. It's hard to tell trustworthy guidance from a get-rich-quick pitch.

🇸🇬

Generic answers don't fit

Most content online is US-focused. Young Singaporeans need answers that cover CPF, SRS, SGX and local rules.

⏱️

No time for deep dives

People want a quick, clear answer to one question — not a two-hour course before they can act.

How it works

Ask a question. Get a grounded answer.

MoneyMentor uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG): it looks up a curated knowledge base of trusted Singapore sources before it answers, so replies stay accurate — not made up.
