Choose the route before choosing the product.

Use this page when you are asking, “Which place or platform route fits me?” The quiz below is only for Where to invest, then the cards explain each route clearly.

SGX · do it yourself

The stock market (SGX)

Singapore’s stock exchange, where shares, ETFs and REITs are bought and sold. Best if you want control and are willing to do homework.

Apps that build and manage a diversified portfolio for you based on goals and risk comfort. Good when choosing products yourself feels overwhelming.

Monthly investing plans

Regular Shares Savings plans invest a fixed sum each month into an ETF like the STI ETF. Good for building a calm monthly habit.

Government · safest

Government-backed options

Singapore Savings Bonds and Treasury bills are issued by the Government. Best for money you need soon or cannot afford to lose.
