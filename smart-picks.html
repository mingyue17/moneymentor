S$500 starter recommendation

Default assumption: beginner in Singapore, moderate risk, wants a simple and sensible first step.

  1. 1

    Singapore Savings Bonds

    Top fit when safety matters most. SSBs are backed by the Singapore Government and start from S$500.

    Best for safetyLower growth
  2. 2

    STI ETF or regular ETF savings plan

    Best first stock-market route because it spreads money across many companies instead of one stock.

    Best beginner market exposureMarket can fall
  3. 3

    Robo-advisor portfolio

    Good if the user wants hands-off diversification and does not want to pick individual stocks yet.

    Best hands-off routeFees and risk level matter
  4. 4

    Blue-chip learning candidates

    DBS, OCBC, UOB, Singtel, and REITs are useful learning candidates when daily price/news data supports them.

    Best for learning stocksLess diversified
